A shared stage for established and rising stars

📍 Toulouse Zenith (12,000 seats)

The SKR FESTIVAL brings together 5 K-pop groups: 1 to 2 AB-tier headliners, several promising rookies and French artists will perform on the immersive and choreographed stage of the Zénith.

See more

They’ll be joined by young French dancers selected through auditions, showcasing local emerging talent. One French artist will also be selected via a social media discovery contest to perform alongside invited groups.

Confirmed artist: Serri (former leader of Dal★Shabet)

Une scène partagée entre talents établis et étoiles montantes

📍 Zénith de Toulouse (12 000 places)

Le SKR FESTIVAL réunit 5 groupes K-pop : 1 à 2 têtes d’affiche AB-tier, plusieurs rookies prometteurs ainsi que des artistes français se produiront sur la scène du Zénith de Toulouse, dans un show immersif et chorégraphié.

Voir plus

Les artistes seront accompagnés de jeunes danseurs français sélectionnés via audition, pour valoriser la scène montante locale.
Un(e) artiste français(e) sera également sélectionné(e) à travers un concours de découverte sur les réseaux sociaux, pour performer sur scène avec les groupes invités.

Artiste confirmée : Serri (ex-leader du groupe Dal★Shabet)

The K-Culture Village – 40 immersive booths

📍 Pierre Baudis Center (multi-floor)

40 immersive booths across multiple levels invite visitors to explore the richness of Korean culture through interactive experiences inspired by K-dramas and daily life.

See more

A vibrant space dedicated to Korean culture:
• K-Fashion – Korean & French designers + exclusive show
• K-Beauty – Brands & beauty workshops
• K-pop Zone – Albums, goodies, signings
• Food court – Authentic Korean cuisine & cooking demos
• Tech – VR, interactive K-dramas
• Culture – Calligraphy, hanbok, language, games
• Fan Zone – Photobooth, Random Play Dance, fanzines

Visitors become active participants through interactive activities:
• Singing, dance, cooking, makeup workshops
• Culture/tourism panels
• Meet & greets, games, raffles with K-pop prizes

A living space to discover, test, and enjoy everything Hallyu has to offer.

Voir plus

Un espace vibrant dédié à la culture coréenne :
• K-Fashion – Créateurs coréens & français + défilé exclusif
• K-Beauty – Marques & ateliers soins
• K-pop Zone – Albums, goodies, dédicaces
• Gastronomie – Food court typique & ateliers cuisine

• Fan Zone – Photobooth, Random Play Dance, fanzines

Le public devient acteur de l’événement à travers :

Un espace vivant pour découvrir, tester et acheter tout ce qui fait la richesse de la Hallyu.

K-컬처 빌리지 – 몰입형 부스 40개

📍 피에르 보디 센터 (다층 공간)

피에르 보디 센터의 여러 층에 걸친 40개의 부스에서, K-드라마와 한국 일상을 주제로 한 다양한 활동과 체험을 통해 한국 문화를 깊이 있게 경험할 수 있습니다.

더 보기

모든 K-컬처를 만날 수 있는 생동감 넘치는 공간:
• K-패션 – 한불 디자이너 및 독점 패션쇼
• K-뷰티 – 브랜드 & 뷰티 워크숍
• K-팝 존 – 앨범, 굿즈, 사인회
• 푸드코트 – 전통 음식 및 쿠킹 클래스
• 기술 – VR, 인터랙티브 K-드라마
• 문화 – 서예, 한복, 한국어, 전통놀이
• 팬존 – 포토부스, 랜덤 플레이 댄스, 팬진

관람객이 직접 참여하는 인터랙티브 활동:
• 노래, 춤, 요리, 메이크업 워크숍
• 문화/관광 패널
• 팬사인회, 게임, K팝 경품 추첨

한류를 직접 체험하고 즐길 수 있는 살아있는 공간입니다.
