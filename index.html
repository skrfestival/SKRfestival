Who Are We?

SKR Festival is a cultural initiative founded by a collective passionate about Korean culture. Our mission is to promote the Hallyu wave through an immersive, inclusive and festive event open to all. We believe in the power of cultural bridges and in giving voice to the talents shaping K-culture in Europe and Asia.

Our core values are authenticity, celebration, diversity, and innovation. With each edition, we strive to strengthen the cultural link between Korea and the European youth, particularly in the South of France.

Festival Program

SKR Festival unfolds over a vibrant weekend dedicated to the Hallyu wave. Attendees will enjoy:

  • K-Village: A cultural market with food, fashion, and Korean games.
  • Workshops: Dance, calligraphy, hanbok fitting, and language discovery.
  • After Party: Featuring Korean DJs and themed activities.

Why Toulouse?

Toulouse stands as a strategic, dynamic and culturally rich European city. With modern infrastructures, international exposure and a vibrant youth community, it provides the perfect backdrop for SKR Festival.

As the 4th largest city in France and a hub for aerospace, tech, and culture, Toulouse ensures logistical convenience, strong media coverage and significant tourist potential.
