Why Toulouse?

Toulouse stands as a strategic, dynamic and culturally rich European city. With modern infrastructures, international exposure and a vibrant youth community, it provides the perfect backdrop for SKR Festival.

As the 4th largest city in France and a hub for aerospace, tech, and culture, Toulouse ensures logistical convenience, strong media coverage and significant tourist potential.
